(Game) Das Wahre Schlagerfest

 

Das Wahre Schlagerfest was programmed on the 30th September 2003 during a 'Spass programmieren' session. It is the one game that can be played on a theme party with theme 'Germany', or as a substitute for the Oktoberfest. If you want a beer or bratwurst, you can just go fetching one without fear of losing the game!

 

Initially, Das Wahre Schlagerfest was programmed in C++ using the C++ Builder 6.0 IDE, for a Windows-only version, but in 2012 it was ported to Qt Creator.
