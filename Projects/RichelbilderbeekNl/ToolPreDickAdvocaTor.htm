(Tool) PreDickAdvocaTor

 

PreDickAdvocaTor is a tool to predict the soccer world championship scores of 2010.

 

PreDickAdvocaTor is released at the 30th of May 2010 and is programmed using the Qt Creator IDE, using the STL and QT libraries.
