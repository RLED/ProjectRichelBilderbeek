(C++) Erich Gamma

 

Erich Gamma is one of 'The Gang Of Four' that wrote 'Design Patterns' [0] , which is/was one of the most important C++ books [1] .

 

 

 

 

 

List of books co-authored by Erich Gamma

*        Erich Gamma, Richard Helm, Ralph Johnson, John Vlissides. Design Patterns. 1995. ISBN: 0201633612.
