(Game) SpaceHarry

 

Space Harry is a simple 'Space Harrier' clone.

 

Harry was one of my favorite bartenders in one of my favorite (now closed) pubs.

 

Space Harry is programmed in C++ using the IDE C++ Builder 6.0. It uses the VCL and Boost libraries.

Game Controls

 

  • Arrows: move Harry
  • Space: fire
  • F1: toggle transparency mode
