(C++) John Vlissides

 

John Vlissides was one of 'The Gang Of Four' that wrote 'Design Patterns' [0] , which is/was one of the most important C++ books [1] .

 

John Vlissides died on November the 24th of 2005.

 

 

 

 

 

List of books co-authored by John Vlissides

*        Erich Gamma, Richard Helm, Ralph Johnson, John Vlissides. Design Patterns. 1995. ISBN: 0201633612.
